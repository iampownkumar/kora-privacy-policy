Privacy Policy

How Kora handles your data

Effective date: April 2, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 2, 2026

The short version: Kora Launcher is built local-first. Your app usage stats, flagged apps, daily limits, to-do tasks, and intentions stay entirely on your device. Kora does not collect, upload, or sell any personal data. The only outbound network requests are font loading (Google Fonts) and update checks. No accounts. No analytics. No tracking.

👤 About this app

Kora Launcher ("Kora", "we", "us") is an Android home screen replacement app developed by Pownkumar. It is available on the Google Play Store under the package name org.korelium.koralauncher.

This Privacy Policy describes what permissions Kora requests, why they are needed, and how any related data is handled.

Contact: iampownkumar@gmail.com

🔒 Data Kora does not collect

Kora does not collect, store on any server, or share any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any account information
  • Your location
  • Your contacts or call history
  • Photos, camera, or microphone data
  • Your app usage stats or screen time data
  • Your flagged apps, to-do list, goals, or daily intentions
  • Any behavioural, advertising, or analytics data

All app data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server owned or operated by Kora.

🔑 Permissions and why we use them

Below is every permission Kora requests and a plain-language explanation of why it is needed.

📊
Usage Access
PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS
Allows Kora to read app usage statistics from Android. This powers the Usage Dashboard and the Rising Tide daily limit system, which tracks your progress against the time budgets you set per app.

This data is read from the Android system and processed entirely on your device. It is never uploaded, logged to a server, or shared with anyone.
✦ Optional feature
♿
Accessibility Service
BIND_ACCESSIBILITY_SERVICE
Allows Kora to detect when you open a different app on your device. This is the technical foundation of the Rising Tide focus system, which shows a brief interception screen before a distracting app opens.

Kora uses the Accessibility Service only for this one purpose: detecting app foreground transitions for apps you have personally flagged. It does not read screen content, keystrokes, passwords, or any sensitive data. It is not used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than Rising Tide interception.

This permission is optional. If you do not enable it, the rest of Kora — launcher, app drawer, to-do list, and usage dashboard — continues to work normally.
✦ Optional feature
🏠
Default Launcher
android.intent.category.HOME
Declares Kora as a home screen to Android. This allows you to set Kora as your default launcher, replacing your phone's stock home screen. No personal data is involved in this declaration.
Core feature
📱
Query All Packages
QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
Allows Kora to read the list of apps installed on your device. This is required for any launcher to populate the App Drawer and to let you choose which apps to flag for Rising Tide. The app list is used only locally and is never transmitted off your device.
Core feature
🖼️
Set Wallpaper
SET_WALLPAPER
Allows Kora to change your Android home screen wallpaper when you use the wallpaper picker feature inside the app. No image data is collected or uploaded.
✦ Optional feature
🌐
Internet
INTERNET
Used for two purposes only:
  • Font loading: Kora loads a typeface from Google Fonts for the launcher UI.
  • Update checks: Kora checks for app updates from the Play Store.

No personal data, usage stats, or identifying information is included in these requests.
Core feature

🔗 Third-party services

Kora uses the following third-party services in limited ways:

  • Google Fonts — Font loading for the launcher UI. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Play — Standard in-app update availability check. See Google's Privacy Policy.

Kora does not use Firebase, Crashlytics, AdMob, Facebook SDK, or any analytics or advertising SDK.

🗄️ Data safety

All user data created in Kora — including your to-do tasks, daily intentions, flagged apps, daily limits, and app usage stats — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (Hive / SharedPreferences).

This data is not backed up to any cloud service by Kora, not transmitted to any Kora server, and not shared with any third party.

When you uninstall Kora, all locally stored data is deleted by Android as part of the standard uninstall process.

👶 Children's privacy

Kora Launcher is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children. If you believe your child has provided information to this app, please contact us and we will take steps to address it.

If we make any material changes to this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of Kora Launcher after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
