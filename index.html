Privacy Policy

Kora Launcher — A launcher that asks why.

Effective Date: March 24, 2026  ·  Last Updated: March 25, 2026  · 

✦  Kora has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics. Every piece of data the app generates lives on your device and nowhere else.

1. Who We Are

Kora Launcher is an Android home screen replacement application built by Pownkumar (Founder of Korelium). The app is designed to help users build conscious habits around smartphone usage through behavioral awareness — not surveillance.

2. What Data Kora Stores

All data described below is stored exclusively in a local SQLite database on your device. It is never uploaded, synced, or transmitted anywhere.

Data stored on your device
  • App usage duration — how many minutes per day you spend in each app, read from Android's UsageStats API and stored locally for display in the Usage Dashboard.
  • Daily intentions — the text you type each morning as your personal goal for the day. Stored locally, visible only to you.
  • Behavioral decisions — whether you chose to open or resist a flagged app at the interception screen, and the reason category you selected (e.g. "Habit / Boredom"). Used to show you your own resistance patterns over time.
  • Session timestamps — when a flagged app was opened and when you returned to the launcher. Used to calculate session duration.
  • Tasks — the to-do items you enter on the home screen. Stored in SharedPreferences on your device.
  • Flagged app list — which apps you have chosen to place behind the interception gate.

3. What Kora Does Not Collect

  • Your name, email address, phone number, or any identity information
  • The content of your messages, emails, photos, or any other app
  • Your location, GPS coordinates, or IP address
  • Contacts, call logs, or calendar data
  • Advertising identifiers or device fingerprints
  • Crash reports or diagnostic data sent to any external service

Kora contains no advertising SDKs, no analytics SDKs, no Firebase, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

4. Permissions Explained

Permissions requested by Kora
📊
PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS
Allows Kora to read how much time you spend in each app today. This powers the Usage Dashboard. The data is read-only, stored locally, and never transmitted. You grant this manually in Android Settings — Kora cannot access it without your explicit action.
📱
QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES
Required for any Android launcher to enumerate and display installed apps. Without this, Kora cannot show your app drawer or function as a home screen. Only app names, icons, and package identifiers are read — no app content is accessed.
🖼️
SET_WALLPAPER
Allows Kora to manage the home screen appearance. Used only for visual customisation of the launcher background.
🌐
INTERNET
Required to check for app updates via the in-app update checker, and to load Google Fonts for the launcher UI. No personal data is transmitted over this connection. Kora does not communicate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

5. Data Sharing

Kora does not share any data with any third party, ever. There are no advertising partners, analytics providers, data brokers, or cloud services involved in the operation of this app.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

All data is stored locally on your device for as long as the app is installed. Uninstalling Kora permanently and completely deletes all stored data — intentions, sessions, decisions, tasks, and app usage records. There is no server-side data, so there is nothing additional to request deletion of.

7. Children's Privacy

Kora is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used this app, uninstalling it will remove all associated data from the device.

8. Security

Because Kora has no servers and no network transmission of user data, there is no risk of a server-side data breach. All data resides in Android's standard application storage directory, which is sandboxed and inaccessible to other apps by default.

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised effective date at the top. Continued use of Kora after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
